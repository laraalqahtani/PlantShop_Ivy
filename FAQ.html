FAQ

Frequently asked Questions
How will my plant arrive?

We aim to deliver your plant in a healthy condition. Your plant will be sitting comfortably in its nursery grow pot from the moment it leaves our greenery until it reaches your home. This way, your plant will be relaxed and joyfull as opposing to depotting it and potting it, and risking the plant going into shock and withering.


My plant arrived healthy but now it's seems to be wilting.
What should I do?

You can easily reach out to us with photos by contacting us through email or phone number found in the Contact section. We'll be happy to help!


My plant arrived unhealthy/damaged. What should I do?

We always do our best to make sure you plant reaches you in a healthy condition. If not, we will make sure to send you a new one free of expenses! Damage beyond repair ensures a replacement. Reach out to us with photos within 30 Days of receipt for a free plant replacement.


Do I have to repot my plant as soon as it arrives?

No. You have to firstly allow your plant to adapt to its new environment by setting it in the spot you plan on keeping it in for about two weeks. Once two the weeks have passed, you can then repot it as you like. If you wish for your plant to grow bigger, make sure to repot it in a larger pot so it can thrive in a spacious home!


Why does my plant look different from the website?

While we do our best to show our plants as accuratley as they are in real life, a slight difference may appear. Plants vary in size, color, shape, and feel. The travel conditions may slightly change your plant's appearence but it can be restored easily by care.
